About OceanHacks

Welcome to OceanHacks, a 12-hour student-led hackathon in the Los Angeles area.

Students of all skill levels are welcome!
No experience is necessary. Mentors will help teach you!

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

Any high schooler or middle schooler can participate!

Does OceanHacks cost money?

No! OceanHacks is completely free to attend!

Additionally, Hack Club has generously offered to reimburse the cost of gas for you (or your parent) to drive to OceanHacks! This school year, we'll fund the cost of gas for you (or a parent) to drive to any hackathon listed on hackathons.hackclub.com. They will alternatively reimburse you the equivalent of that if you buy a train or bus ticket! You can find more information on their website: gas.hackclub.com.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

We will provide food for all dietary restrictions listed when you register! Make sure to let us know dietary restrictions through email or when registering or there is no guarantee we will be able to accommodate! 

What should I bring?

A form of identification (Student ID, Drivers License, etc.), laptop, charger(s), and any other items that you think you'll need!

How are teams formed?

Teams of up to 4 are allowed! Teams can be formed before the event, or we can help you find a team at the beginning of the event!
